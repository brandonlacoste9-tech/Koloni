Frequently Asked Questions

What are AI Credits?

AI Credits are used to generate content. Each LongCat or Emu generation uses 1 credit. Credits never expire and roll over month to month.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets through Stripe's secure payment processing.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All new accounts start with 10 free credits to try out the platform. No credit card required to sign up.
